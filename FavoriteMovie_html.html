Plot Summary

The main character of the series, Gon; a twelve-year-old boy; who goes on an adventure to become a hunter and to look for his dad. He is raised by his aunt and has never met his parents or never left the island he lives in. On his journey, he makes three close friends and faces challenges with them despite having their own different goals. Through his journey, Gon slowly realizes that his father planned his future beforehand.

Recommendation

I am a fan of long season, good quality anime with great animation and story. Hunter x Hunter is a perfect mix of the two. Anime fans will tell you that the show might have ended, but no one has forgotten the experience to this day. I recommend to everyone to watch this anime who want good quality animation and good story in animes because it offers both.

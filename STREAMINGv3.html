Chrunchyroll has the most anime collection of any other streaming sevices. You can discover new animes all the time in chrunchyroll. It even has a free version for those who don't want to pay.

Tubi TV

Tubi TV is a free streaming service platform. Tubi TV doesn't have most of the new movies and TV Shows coming out, so that could be dissappointing. Although, they have quite a popular collection whih is much more impressive than it sounds.

Youtube

Youtube is the world's biggest video streaming platform. Not only is it free, but Youtube works with all devices and browsers making it useful everywhere. It is filled with entertainment and infotainment videos.

Roku TV

Roku TV connects with other streaming services to find users free and paid shows and movies to watch. The TV has its own channel called The Roku Channel. The Channel has many free timelessly popular videos of old and new timeline.
